## 6. Strategy Class / Webinar (The Core Conversion Event)

**Duration**: 60-90 minutes

### Structure

**Part 1: Value (40 mins)**
- UPSC exam pattern breakdown
- Common mistakes aspirants make
- How to plan first 90 days
- Subject prioritization strategy
- Answer writing fundamentals

**Part 2: Introduce GS Foundation (15 mins)**
- What makes Anantam different
- Course structure walkthrough
- Faculty introduction
- Success stories (rankers)

**Part 3: Offer + Urgency (10 mins)**
- Special launch pricing
- Limited seats (batch size matters for mentorship)
- Bonuses for early enrollers
- Payment plans available

**Part 4: Q&A (15 mins)**
- Live doubt clearing
- Build trust and rapport

### Key Conversion Elements
- Show course dashboard/portal (visual proof)
- Student testimonials (video clips)
- Ranker interviews (credibility)
- Limited-time offer (urgency)
- Easy payment options (EMI, installments)

## Success Factors

1. **Trust Building**: UPSC is a long journey - students need to trust the institute
2. **Value First**: Give before you ask (free content, strategy, guidance)
3. **Faculty Credibility**: Highlight experience and ranker track record
4. **Community Feel**: Make them feel part of something bigger
5. **Clear Differentiation**: Why Anantam vs others?
6. **Urgency Without Pressure**: Limited seats are real in mentorship programs
7. **Multiple Touchpoints**: Email + WhatsApp + Retargeting = higher conversion
