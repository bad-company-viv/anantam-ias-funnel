## Ad Testing Strategy

### A/B Testing Elements

**Headlines:**
- Question vs Statement
- Benefit vs Feature
- Urgency vs Value

**Images:**
- Faculty vs Students
- Single person vs Group
- Professional vs Casual

**CTA:**
- "Register Free" vs "Join Now"
- "Learn More" vs "Get Started"
- "Enroll Now" vs "Secure Your Seat"

**Offers:**
- Free strategy session vs Free checklist
- Discount vs Bonus modules
- Limited seats vs Limited time

### Testing Process

1. Create 3-4 variations
2. Run for 3-5 days
3. Analyze metrics (CTR, CPC, conversion)
4. Keep winner, test new variations
5. Continuous optimization

## Budget & Bidding Strategy

### Starting Budget (Monthly)

**Total: ₹1,00,000/month**

- Google Search: ₹40,000
- Facebook/Instagram: ₹35,000
- YouTube: ₹20,000
- Telegram: ₹5,000

### Bidding Strategy

**Google Ads:**
- Start: Manual CPC
- Target: ₹20-40 per click
- Optimize for: Conversions
- After data: Automated bidding

**Facebook/Instagram:**
- Objective: Lead generation
- Optimization: Landing page views
- Bid: Lowest cost
- After data: Cost cap

**YouTube:**
- Bid: CPV (Cost per view)
- Target: ₹2-5 per view
- Optimize for: View rate

### Scaling Strategy

**Week 1-2: Testing**
- Budget: ₹50,000
- Goal: Test creatives, audiences
- Metric: CTR, CPC

**Week 3-4: Optimization**
- Budget: ₹75,000
- Goal: Optimize winners
- Metric: Conversion rate

**Week 5+: Scaling**
- Budget: ₹1,00,000+
- Goal: Scale what works
- Metric: ROI, ROAS

## Key Metrics to Track

### Traffic Metrics
- Impressions
- Clicks
- CTR (Click-through rate)
- CPC (Cost per click)

### Conversion Metrics
- Landing page conversion rate
- Cost per lead
- Webinar registration rate
- Webinar attendance rate
- Enrollment rate
- Cost per enrollment

### ROI Metrics
- ROAS (Return on ad spend)
- Customer acquisition cost
- Lifetime value
- Payback period

### Target Benchmarks
- CTR: 2-5% (Google), 1-3% (Facebook)
- Landing page conversion: 20-30%
- Webinar attendance: 30-40%
- Webinar conversion: 10-20%
- Cost per lead: ₹100-300
- Cost per enrollment: ₹2,000-5,000
